Introduction to bitcoin & Bitcoin

If you are new to bitcoin, your first question might be “what is the difference between bitcoin & Bitcoin?” Do not worry, everything will be explained in this post on Introduction to bitcoin & Bitcoin.

I was a skeptic for a long time and only recently (Summer 2020) started reading everything I could about bitcoin and fell down the rabbit hole.

In this post, I will share my learning path in hopes to help others understand too.

Answer to question: bitcoin is money and Bitcoin is the monetary network.
